Workflow

Workflow

From extant literature we have found a novel molecule crucial to CSFB activity and that of other pests, with no homology found in pollinators or humans. This molecule will serve as the receptor in our workflow and be referred to as Protein A.

Using characterised crystal structures and AlphaFold predictions we will rent cloud GPU computing to run BindCraft (de novo protein design software) to produce a large panel of potential protein binders (Protein B).

The panel of potential Protein B binders will be filtered and refined. They will then be sent to a CRO (Contract Research Organisation) to run binding affinity tests.

Binders (Protein B) with high affinity to the receptor (Protein A) will be optimised to increase binding affinity. They will then be expressed at scale for insect assay testing.
